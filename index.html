About Lab à Mémé

Since 2023

Lab à Mémé is dedicated to providing high-quality food and service. Our journey started in 2023 with a passion for culinary excellence and a desire to create memorable dining experiences. Our team of skilled chefs uses only the finest ingredients to craft dishes that are both delicious and visually stunning. Join us to experience the best in gourmet dining.

Our mission is to deliver exceptional culinary experiences that delight the senses. We believe in the power of food to bring people together and create lasting memories. Whether you're joining us for a casual meal or a special occasion, we strive to make every visit extraordinary.

Discover the Art of Flavor at Our Bakery!

Savor our Sausage Bun with corn and cheese, enjoy the warmth of Ginger Oat Scones, and explore six delightful Financier varieties—from black sesame to gluten-free chocolate hazelnut.

Upgrade Your Snacks!

Delight in the rich, nutty Fig Pumpkin Seed and the irresistible chocolate-dipped Nurunji. Every bite is a crunch of pure joy!

Satisfy Your Sweet Tooth!

Try our walnut and brown sugar-stuffed Hotteok or indulge in 'THE' cookie, packed with 64% dark chocolate and white chocolate morsels.

Treat Yourself to Our Unique Bars!

Delight in the nutty Hodu or the rich, gluten-free Korean sweet potato brownie. Pure bliss in every bite!

Roll into Deliciousness with Our Kimbap!

Enjoy a variety of flavors, from Tofu and Tuna to Simili Crab and Bulgogi—every roll is a tasty delight!

Dig into Deopbap!

Savor hearty bowls topped with Chicken, Pork Jeyuk, or Beef Bulgogi—comfort food at its finest!

Bite into Fun with Our Katsu Sando!

Enjoy a cat-shaped bread filled with crispy katsu fried chicken. It’s a delightful twist on a classic sandwich!

Discover Delicious Side Dishes and Refreshing Drinks!

From radishes and Jorim delights to hot teas, flavored lattes, and sparkling fruit teas. Click on the menu button to see all our offerings!
